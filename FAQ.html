FAQ

Where do we ship to?

Our company proudly offers worldwide shipping services, ensuring that customers from all corners of the globe can enjoy our products delivered straight to their doorstep, regardless of their location or geographical boundaries.

How long does the shipping take?

The estimated time for delivery of our shipments typically ranges between 3 to 7 days, varying depending on the geographical location of our valued customers. This timeframe ensures that regardless of where you are located, whether it be near or far, we strive to promptly deliver your order to your doorstep within a reasonable and expedient timeframe.

How frequently do we refresh our inventory of vinyl records?

We make it a priority to update our supply of vinyl records frequently, typically on a weekly basis. This ensures that our inventory remains dynamic and diverse, offering our customers a steady stream of new and exciting options to explore and enjoy.

Which payment methods are accepted by our company?

We accept payments via debit cards, credit cards, and invoices processed through KLARNA.
